About Us

India Must Change Its Approach to Blood Donation. Even today, India is heavily dependent on replacement blood donation, wherein family or friends are asked to donate blood in lieu of the units given to a patient. However, this form of blood donation does not necessarily replace the blood group or quantity administered and leads to increased risk of transfusion-transmissible infections, and this is where we come in. Our website intends to revolutionize the ease of donating blood and making it accessible to anyone and everyone in need.
